Definitions

Object
An object is anything presented on loc.gov with a https://www.loc.gov/item/ URL. An object could be a map set or volume with hundreds or even thousands of map sheets, or a single map.
Online
Items where digitized:true. Includes digitized materials, webpages, finding aids, e-resources, other online resources. Example search (all online materials from LOC): https://www.loc.gov/search/?fa=digitized:true
Not online
Items where digitized:false. Includes links to catalog and other descriptive records (other than finding aids) but objects themselves aren't online. Example search (all not online materials from LOC): https://www.loc.gov/search/?fa=digitized:false
GMD.MAR
Items where group:gmd.mar. GMD.MAR is a grouping for internal processing purposes. It holds all online materials from LOC's Geography and Map Division that are not otherwise held in another processing group. Note that these materials may not necessarily be maps. All GMD.MAR items are online. Example search (all GMD.MAR materials): https://www.loc.gov/search/?sq=group:gmd.mar.
Maps
Items where original-format:map. Example search (all maps from LOC): https://www.loc.gov/search/?fa=original-format:map, equivalent to https://www.loc.gov/maps
Sanborns
Items where partof:sanborn. Example search (all Sanborns): https://www.loc.gov/collections/sanborn-maps/, equivalent to https://www.loc.gov/search/?fa=partof:sanborn maps
